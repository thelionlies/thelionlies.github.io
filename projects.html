Technical Projects

  • TDR LSTM Based Autoencoders A Tagalog Diacritic Restoration project that uses LSTM-based autoencoders to restore missing diacritics in homographs. The work includes experimental modeling and evaluation using text data from the Sari-Samot blogs of Virgilio Almario.
  • TDR Transformer Based Models A transformer-based Tagalog Diacritic Restoration project focused on improving restoration quality through corpus expansion, linguistic analysis, and model experimentation.
  • FIES Viewer A Django-based web application for exploring and analyzing household economic data from the Family Income and Expenditure Survey (FIES) in the CALABARZON Region, Philippines, with tools for interactive data browsing and analysis.
  • SIMS: Social Impact Mapping Simulation A Java-based two-party election simulation grounded in Social Impact Theory (Nowak, Szamrej, & Latané, 1990), featuring a GUI and dynamic modeling of social influence in voting behavior.
