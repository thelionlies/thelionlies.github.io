Note on Some Works

René is a collection of digitally manipulated self-portraits in which the artist parodies famous works by Magritte. Each digital illustration is accompanied by a poem that meditates on the process of editing.

Ang Pagtupad ng mga Robot sa Tatlong Batas ng Robotics was published in Kalat: Palihang LIRA Batch 37, Heights 70(2), and Metro Meka Mega.

21st Century Maligno was published in Kalat: Palihang LIRA Batch 37 and Metro Meka Mega.
